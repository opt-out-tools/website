Vision

At Opt Out Tools, affectionately known as OOT, we're building tech to help everyone with something to say to participate in healthy online discussion safely.

We want social media platforms to be a respectful place where anyone can comment without fear if misogynistic backlash. We want online debate to be inclusive so all voices can flourish. We want social networks to be safer places for all.

We want to achieve this by building tools and an organization with consent, privacy and transparency at their core.

Values

We align with the feminist principles of the internet, but specifically champion the values below.

  • Transparency

    We want to pioneer transparent tech, communicating our decisions and limitations as clearly as possible

  • Respect

    We want fruitful online conversations to prosper and misogynistic harassment to die down

  • Not Censorship

    We will not decide what misogyny is nor will you, both we and you get a say

  • Inclusivity

    We want all respectful voices to flourish

  • Open source

    Because being safe online should be available to everyone

  • Security

    Ensuring that an individual is safe is top priority

Team

Maintainers

At Opt Out Tools, maintainers are the people who have committed to moving the project forward, collaborating relentlessly on Slack and during our weekly hack nights at Mozilla Berlin! Meet some of the core members of the team behind the technology!

Teresa Ingram, Opt Out Tools founder

A cyber activist currently working to help make the internet accessible for all. Teresa came into the tech industry after a degree in Chemistry and a first job as a Java Developer for a bank. As the only woman in a man-club team, Teresa understood the struggle of women being silenced offline and she decided to build something that could empower women on the internet!

Cheuk Ting Ho

After doing research in theoretical physics, Cheuk has transferred her analytical and logical skills in natural science and built a career in data science. After having a career as a Data Scientist, Cheuk now brings her knowledge in data and passion for the tech community into TerminusDB as the developer relations lead.

Cheuk constantly contributes to the open-source community by giving AI and deep learning workshops and organize sprints to encourage contributions. Cheuk has also been a guest speaker at various conferences and co-organize AI Club for Gender Minorities to support Diversity and Inclusion.

Cheuk join the Opt Out Tools team hoping we could have a more inclusive internet for everyone.

Dr. Matteo Guzzo

A Physicist by training, Matteo is a freelance Data Scientist. He's an avid learner and ready to explore all unexpected things, related to Data or not. An organizer of the local meetup PyBerlin, he tries to help the community by making it an inclusive and safe space. 

Andrada Pumnea

Andrada's biggest passion is using Data Science to decrease the digital divide; hate speech detection against female-identifying people is a big part of this. To Andrada, language defines our experience of the internet. To make the internet more inclusive and avoid people being excluded from important conversations, Andrada wants to contribute to the development of language technology that is fair and accurate for any language, especially when it comes to detecting and combating hate speech online.
